About/Acerda De

Mission Statement/Declaracion de Mision

Student Board is a program that's across most DPS schools, in order to better student voice. Lincoln's Student Board was started as a class at Lincoln High School in the 2015/2016 school year. Student Board's mission, is to become an outlet for students. By outlet, I mean the voice of the students. We strive to be effective representatives for students. We want students to feel like, and know, that their voice matters. To add to that, we want to establish a better relationship between students, teachers, admin, and most importantly, the community.

La Tabla de Estudiantes es un programa implementado en la mayoria de las escuelas en DPS para mejorar voz de estudiantes. La Tabla de Estudiantes de la Lincoln High School fue establecido en el año escolar de 2015/2016. La declaracion de mision para la Tabla de Estudiantes, es para ser una salida efectiva para estudiantes. Nos esforzamos pa ser representantes efectivos para estudiantes. Queremos que estudiantes sienten, y sepan, que sus voces importan. Añadir a eso, queremos establecer mejor communicacion sobre estudiantes, maestros, admin, y lo mas importante, la comunidad.
